# AR Photo

Let people take a photo right inside your AR experience and share it in a single tap. The snapshot blends the real world seen through the camera with your 3D content, so visitors capture the moment exactly as they see it — your model sitting on their table, in their room, out in the world — and send it straight to friends, socials, or messages.

It's a wonderful way to turn a quick AR try-on into something people actually keep and share, giving your product a little extra reach every time someone snaps a picture.

## How it works

Two ready-made pieces do the heavy lifting, no custom code needed:

- [**WebARCameraBackground**](https://engine.needle.tools/docs/api/WebARCameraBackground) shows the live camera feed behind your scene and makes sure that real-world view is included in the photo.
- [**screenshot2**](https://engine.needle.tools/docs/api/screenshot2) captures the composited image and hands it to the device's native share sheet, so sharing feels just like sharing any other photo.

Learn more in the [WebXR documentation](https://engine.needle.tools/docs/how-to-guides/xr/).
