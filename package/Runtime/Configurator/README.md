# Configurator

Build a 3D product configurator for the web — let customers switch between product variants and swap materials like colors, finishes, and paint jobs in real time, right in the browser. Product configurators are one of the most popular uses of 3D on the web.

This sample demonstrates the two building blocks: switching **objects** on and off to show product variants, and swapping **materials** on a model. It runs on desktop, mobile, and XR, and you can drive it from your own UI. Pair it with samples like Hotspots or VR Hands for richer input.
